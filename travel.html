Nerd Travel Blog

Last Update: December 07, 2019

I travel with the people respected (read labeled) as Nerds . We generally discuss beauty of places in terms of their location from the Mean in Gaussian Distribution . For instance, if we want to say place is rare, someone will say this place lies beyond three sigma (If you can't get it, don't worry. It is just a mathematical definition of rare). We rank food as Second Best from top and bottom. Therefore, the travel blog may sound like research paper at times.

Phi Phi: Adventures of Colored Waters

I traveled with three friends of mine to the Phi Phi Islands in Thailand. This is the same island cluster where movie The Beach (2000) was shot and created an ecosystem issue . The trip was planned to do Snorkeling and Scuba Diving. We did enjoy them greatly with nature loving Blue View Divers . The blog is written primarily by my friend Juhi and further edited by Sankaran and me.

Penang: A Street Art Gallery

I visited Penang, Malaysia , with Juhi and Vijesh. The George Town is a UNESCO designated World Heritage Site and big beautiful street art gallery itself.
